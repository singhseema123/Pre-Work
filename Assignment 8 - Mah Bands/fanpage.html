Who is Still Woozy?

Sven Gamsky, known professionally as Still Woozy, is an American singer and songwriter. He combines electronic and acoustic elements to produce a unique style that he calls “wooz”.
He gained public recognition after releasing Cooks (2017), Vacation (2017), Wolfcat (2017), Goodie Bag (2017), and Lucy (feat. Odie) (2018).[1]
